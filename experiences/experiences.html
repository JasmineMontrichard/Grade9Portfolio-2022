Experiences

My experience as a first-year high school student has been amazing. I met a lot of new people and my enrollment into an early college program has gave me a lot of opportunities.

Below is a brief timeline of the highlighted experiences I have had this year.

My Grade 9 P-TECH Experience

At the start of July, all new Grade 9 students in SJB's P-TECH program were invited to the virtual Summer CODEfest 2021. This event provided students the chance to get to know each other and participate in activites that give a brief introduction to HTML/CSS. There was also a weekly draw for the month, where the winner would receive a 300$ gift card of their choice. I really liked that we had a beginner's grasp at what we would learn in our P-TECH courses.

Throughout the school year, my P-TECH class and I learned math, web development, Java, and hardware. Our courses were fast-paced to make sure we get our college diploma before graduating high school. Although I enjoy learning what the P-TECH program has to offer, I find that it also means you will also not be getting as many electives as usual. Overall, I enjoy being in the program and I am not planning to leave anytime soon!

Programmania

April 2022

This was my first programming competition. This was organized by other secondary school business and computer departments within our school board. Most of the participants were in Grades 10-12 while few were in Grade 9. All teams that represented each school had 3 hours to complete 4 problems in the quickest time. I was kind of bummed out I only had one teammate but this was our first time competiting in a programming competition.

I learned that I would need to prepare a lot more especially since I was going up against those who have programmed for more than a year now. However, this contest gave me more experience and a reminder to keep practising. Although we did not win, I enjoyed representing the school and I am willing to come back next year for the next competition!
